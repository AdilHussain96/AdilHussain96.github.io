About

Research profile

I develop AI and machine learning pipelines for digital health applications, with experience across biomedical data analysis, clinical decision support, voice and audio biomarkers, medical image analysis, and multimodal data integration.

My background includes international research collaboration, EU project work, grant and proposal support, prototype planning, student supervision, and research-to-product translation for healthcare technologies.
